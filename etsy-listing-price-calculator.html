      Etsy Base Fee Calculations

      These have an impact on the overall fee totals, but these actual amounts will vary based on individual order.

      The different fees that are applied to an Etsy order are as follows:

      • Listing fee - $0.20
      • Processing fee - 3% of order total (including sales tax) + $0.25
      • Transaction fee - 6.5% of item total (listed price - minus any applied coupons)
      • Shipping Label Transaction fee - 6.5% of shipping label total

      (While this varies state to state, the national average is 7.52%.)
      Etsy Offsite Ad Fee Rate

      need a quick line here

      Etsy will automatically advertise your listing on your behalf across social media and other channels. If someone clicks an ad and makes a purchase from your shop within 30 days a fee will be applied to your account. The percentage will be based off the total purchase amount, including shipping, minus taxes.

      If you have made less than $10,000 in a year, your ad fee will be 15%. You can disable the external advertising to avoid this, however this option is also tied into search engine results and can limit your listings appearing in normal search results (e.g. Google).

      After you make more than $10,000 in a year the fee will be 12% and you can not disable the option.
